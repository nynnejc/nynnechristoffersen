Open Coding Lab

Open Coding Lab is a space where you are invited to bring in a coding project you want to get support on, or just to hack away in good company with snacks. We are happy to help with all and any kinds of projects, but are also more than welcome to absolute beginners. If you do not have a project to bring, you can also be matched with a learning project or tutorial that fits with your interest and goals.
